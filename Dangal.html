Let's make Sel Roti, Nepalese ring-shaped sweet rice bread.

Utensils needed to make Sel Roti:

  • Deep/flat base frying pan
  • Two sticks (cuptero)- 1 foot long
  • A cup to pour mixture in pan
  • Plate to keep Sel Roti

Ingredients to make Sel Roti

  • Rice - 2.5 Pound (or instant rice flour from Walmart)
  • Water or milk - 500 ml
  • Ghee or Butter - 2 cups
  • Sugar - 2 cups
  • Cooking Oil - 1 liter

Recipe for making Sel Roti:

  1. Wash and soak rice overnight, drain excess water.
  2. Mix ghee/butter and sugar and grind into fine paste. The paste should be fine and greasy.
  3. Continuously stir the mixture
  4. Cover it and leave at the room temperature for 1-2 hours to melt and mix all the ingredients.
  5. Heat the pan with cooking oil. The pan should be deep enough to float sel roti and base should be flat.
  6. Watch for vapor/smoke from the oil or see the picking stick float on the oil.
  7. Pour the not too thick batter as continuous ring into hot oil til they become brown/golden.
  8. Confirm both sides are brown
  9. Take those sel roti and serve as breakfast or you can eat it anytime of the day.
